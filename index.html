Leadership takeaways Events work best when paired with sales follow-up. A meaningful share of qualified opportunities were moved forward by BDR Event, BDR Email, or ABM Target Account, not by passive event sourcing alone. Gartner and SSOW are the best portfolio anchors. They combine value, volume, and conversion. Executive dinners are a conversion accelerator. They generate fewer leads but a high share of qualified opps, often through BDR or ABM-led motion. Not all high-volume events are high-performing. Some events generate names but few qualified opportunities.
