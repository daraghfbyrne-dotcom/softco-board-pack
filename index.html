Partnership Update

Partnership Strategy 2026–2029  ·  February 2026  ·  Confidential

Strategic Objective

Scale partner-sourced and partner-influenced ARR from a nascent channel to >30% of new ARR by 2029, while enabling partners to deliver >80% of implementation days — creating a capital-efficient, diversified growth engine alongside SoftCo's direct sales motion.

Executive Summary

•

The commercial foundations are in place — a structured multi-year agreement with Tungsten Automation, an active referral relationship with Interpath, a time-limited arrangement with Xelix (winding down), and a technology alliance with Sage. Our Microsoft relationship remains under assessment.

•

What has been lacking is governance, enablement infrastructure, and a clear commercial growth trajectory linked to ARR targets. This document addresses that.

Three Strategic Priorities

01

Maximise Existing Partnerships

Implement QBRs, joint business planning, and formal performance tracking with all current partners.

02

Build Referral Ecosystem in 2026

Target two new high-quality referral partners in H1 2026 with structured onboarding and enablement.

03

Lay VAR & SI Foundations from 2027

Build partner infrastructure iteratively — enablement and portal must precede VAR/SI recruitment.
